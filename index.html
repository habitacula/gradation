Blue Lemonade

A refreshing and vibrant blue twist on classic lemonade, perfect for hot summer days. Easy to make and visually stunning.

🕒 10 min
🍋 Easy
❄️ 1 Serve
Ingredients
Fresh Lemon Juice 60ml
Blue Curaçao Syrup 30ml
Simple Syrup 15ml (to taste)
Sparkling Water 150ml
Ice Cubes As needed
Lemon Slices & Mint For Garnish
